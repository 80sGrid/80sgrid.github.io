🏆 World Series Trivia

October 27, 1986. The Mets beat the Red Sox to win it all. But what song was #1 in America that night?

🏆
🎵 Sample Question
What was the #1 song in the USA on October 27, 1986 — the night the Mets won the World Series?
🎯
You're On the Ball!
True Colors by Cyndi Lauper was the #1 song in America the night the Mets closed it out in Game 7. From the album True Colors (1986) — it stayed at #1 for two weeks. The Mets AND Cyndi? New York owned October 1986. ⚾🗽
