MY LOVE LETTER TO 80s MUSIC

Len B / C8O
Chief 80s Officer

I built 80sGrid because the music of the 80s never left me ‚Äî and I had a feeling it never left millions of others either.

This started as a personal obsession and grew into something I never expected: a game that people genuinely don't want to stop playing.

80sGrid is my love letter to 80s music. That love letter takes the form of a music trivia grid game designed to connect and reconnect people with the songs and artists that defined a generation.

From Depeche Mode and Pat Benatar to Prince, Sade, Madonna, The Cure, Michael Jackson, and Elvis Costello, 80s music told the story of my life. It still does. And now a whole new generation is discovering it for the first time.

80sGrid is free, shareable, and built for Gen X, elder Millennials, and younger Boomers who know their 80s. Every day brings a new challenge. Every score gets shared.

"Every day, I write the book." Elvis Costello
