A message from the founder of 80sGrid

1,000 Rainy Days Since We First Met

Junior year

"During my junior year in high school, I had a crush on an older woman. She was a senior."

I somehow found the courage to tell her how I felt. She said she didn't feel the same way.

I went home, up to my bedroom, and listened to "Every Little Thing She Does Is Magic" by The Police probably 30 times in a row.

Many years later, I created this game as a love letter to that music. It's for those of us who lived, breathed, danced, cried, rollerskated and loved during the 80s. Disguised as a free trivia game featuring all the bands and songs that made the decade so special for millions of us.

P.S. This story has a happy ending. During my senior year, she came around. The Eurythmics' "Sweet Dreams Are Made of This" was in heavy rotation.
