PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: February 2026

THE SHORT VERSION

80sGrid is a free trivia game. We don't collect your name, email, or any personal information. We don't sell data. We don't run ads. We just love 80s music and want you to play.

GOOGLE ANALYTICS

We use Google Analytics to understand how people find and use 80sGrid. This collects anonymized data such as pages visited, time on site, and general location (country/city level). No personally identifiable information is collected.

Google Analytics uses cookies to track sessions. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

COOKIES

80sGrid uses cookies and local browser storage to save your daily quiz scores and track whether you have played today. This data stays in your browser and is never sent to us.

Google Analytics also sets its own cookies for session tracking as described above.

THIRD PARTY SERVICES

We use Google Analytics (Google LLC) for site analytics. Google's privacy policy can be found at policies.google.com/privacy.

We do not use any advertising networks, affiliate trackers, or data brokers.

YOUR DATA

We do not collect, store, or sell personal data. Your quiz scores are stored locally in your own browser only. We have no way to identify individual players.
